Comparative Research

Headspace

Headspace aims to help people live a happy and healthy life through meditation. Headspace is available on the web, mobile, and tablet. Headspace’s mobile app is clean, minimal, and enjoyable like their web app. They use color effectively and compliments the content with illustrations. I think the use of illustrations helps make the app more approachable and encourages users to face their stress and anxiety with a more positive mindset.

Visit Headspace

Joyable

Joyable focuses on helping people overcome their social anxiety through a personalized experience with a coach and activities. I couldn’t find a mobile app, but their web app is responsive. The warm and vibrant colors are complimented with friendly typography and hand-drawn icons. As I moved deeper into the site, I felt like some icons and visuals lacked the quality that was present on the front facing marketing pages.

Visit Joyable
